# Compliance Policy — Placeholder

This is a placeholder policy document used for pipeline testing. Replace with the actual policy before production use.

## 1. AML/CFT

The institution maintains an AML/CFT programme in accordance with applicable regulations. Suspicious transaction reports (STRs) shall be filed as soon as practicable upon detection of suspicious activity. A senior AML Compliance Officer is appointed at management level. All staff complete AML awareness training annually.

## 2. Operational Risk

Operational risk is managed through the institution's risk framework. Business continuity plans are tested annually. Incident reporting follows the escalation matrix defined in the Risk Policy.

## 3. Cyber / Information Security

Information security controls follow ISO 27001 principles. Critical systems are subject to annual penetration testing. Cyber incidents are reported to the CISO within 24 hours.

## 4. ICAAP

The institution conducts an Internal Capital Adequacy Assessment Process (ICAAP) annually. Capital adequacy is assessed against Pillar 2 risks including concentration risk, interest rate risk in the banking book, and reputational risk.

## 5. Outsourcing

Material outsourcing arrangements require prior board approval and a written outsourcing agreement covering data protection, audit rights, and exit provisions. The institution retains oversight of all outsourced functions.

## 6. AI Governance

No formal AI governance framework is currently in place. Use of automated decision-making tools is subject to IT risk assessment on a case-by-case basis.
